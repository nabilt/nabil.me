OBD-2 Testing Update

December 24, 2011 #electronics

I finished testing the OBD-II adaptor board this week on a Toyota Camry.
As you can see in the picture, the board had a few bad connections — but nothing a couple of jumpers couldn't fix.

I was able to get a number of stats from the free Torque Android app, including:

  • Throttle position
  • Engine PSI
  • Coolant temperature
  • Engine load
  • A number of diagnostic trouble codes
  • O₂ sensor voltage
  • Revs

One interesting note: the evaporative system test reported as incomplete, even though Toyota supposedly repaired it during my last visit!

Next up: test the remaining OBD-II protocols and Bluetooth range.
Expect a new revision in a few weeks.
